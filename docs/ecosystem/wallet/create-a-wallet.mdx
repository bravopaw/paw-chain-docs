# Creating a PAW Wallet

PAW Wallet allows users to manage digital assets across multiple networks with a simple, secure interface. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to set up your wallet safely and get started.

:::tip

*Your seed phrase is the only way to recover your wallet. If you lose it, your funds are permanently unrecoverable. Always store it securely offline.*

:::


## Minimum Technology Requirements {#min-requirements}

Before setting up PAW Wallet, ensure your device meets the following requirements for smooth performance and security.

- **Mobile**
  - Android 8+ (Recommended: Android 12+)
  - iOS 8+ (Recommended: iOS 16+)
  - Minimum 2GB RAM

- **Desktop (Browser Only)**
  - Chrome, Firefox, Brave, or Edge (latest two versions)
  - Windows 8.1+ or macOS 10.13+ recommended for stability
  - To bridge assets, you will typically connect using an external wallet (e.g., MetaMask)

- **Network**
  - Stable internet connection
  - *Avoid using public Wi-Fi when creating your wallet*

## Secure Your Environment {#secure-your-environment}

Before setting up PAW Wallet, ensure your device meets the following requirements for smooth performance and security.

- Turn off screen sharing or recording apps.
- Cover any external cameras or use a privacy hood.
- Ensure your device is free of malware. (We recommend running an antivirus scan before setup.)
- Don’t set up your wallet in a public place.

## i. Accessing PAW Wallet

Open your in-app wallet browser and visit: `https://wallet.pawchain.net`

## ii. Create Your Wallet

- Agree to the **PAW Wallet Terms of Use**
- Click **Create a new PAW Wallet**

![](/img/wallet1.png)

## iii. Secret Recovery Phrase

- A page will display your **Secret Recovery Phrase**
- Write it down on paper — **do not store it digitally**

![](/img/wallet2.png)

- Optionally click **Copy Seed Phrase**, but still store it offline
- Press **Confirm Phrase**

## iv. Confirm Seed Phrase

- On the next screen, some words will be blanked from the phrase
- Select the correct words in the order they were shown
- Press **Confirm**

## v. Create a Password

- Create a secure password (`8+ characters`, `1 uppercase`, `1 lowercase`, `1 special character`)

![](/img/wallet3.png)

- Press **Create Password**

## vi. Enter the Password

- Re-enter your password on the next screen
- Press **Connect**

# Logging Out

- Click your **PAW Wallet address** in the top-right corner
- Press **Disconnect Wallet**
- To log back in, re-enter your password

![](/img/wallet4.png)

### Secure Log Out

- On the login screen, press **Disconnect**
- This clears the session — you'll need your Secret Recovery Phrase to log back in

![](/img/wallet5.png)

# Logging In with Recovery Phrase

- Press **Recover PAW Wallet** (agree to Terms of Use)
- Enter your Secret Recovery Phrase in order
  - Use lowercase starting letters
  - Typing the first `4` letters will auto-complete each word
- Press **Confirm Secret Recovery Phrase**
- You’ll be prompted to reset your wallet password
- Follow steps **v** and **vi** to regain access
