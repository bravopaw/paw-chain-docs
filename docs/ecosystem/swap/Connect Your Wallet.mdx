# Connect Your Wallet

## Step 1: Accessing PAW Swap

Open your in-app wallet browser and visit `https://swap.pawchain.net`.

## Step 2: Connect Your Wallet

Press the blue **Connect Wallet** button in the upper right-hand corner of the screen.

![](/img/connect-wallet.png)

A pop-up will appear displaying available **EVM** and **non-EVM** chains. At the bottom of the pop-up, press **Skip to Wallet selection**.

Choose your preferred wallet â€” such as **MetaMask**, **WalletConnect**, or another supported option.

Follow the prompts to approve and connect your wallet to **PAW Swap**.

Your wallet is now connected.
