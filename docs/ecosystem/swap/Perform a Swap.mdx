# Performing a Crypto Swap on PAW Swap

## Using the Swap Platform

This guide walks you through how to perform a token swap using the **PAW Swap** platform. Whether you’re converting one asset to another or interacting with cross-chain liquidity, PAW Swap provides a fast, secure, and user-friendly interface for executing your trades.

## i. Access the Swap Interface

Tap the **Swap** option, represented by a circular arrow icon.

## ii. Select Your Tokens

- The top token box is for the asset you are swapping from.
- The bottom token box is for the asset you are receiving.

## iii. Enter Swap Values

- Input the amount you want to swap in the top box.
- The equivalent amount for the receiving token will populate automatically.
- Ensure you have selected the correct slippage setting for your trade — this helps protect against price fluctuations.

## iv. Review Transaction Fee

- The transaction fee will be displayed before executing the swap.
- Confirm you're comfortable with the estimated fee.

## v. Initiate the Swap

Press the **Swap** button at the bottom of the interface.

## vi. Confirm Swap Details

- A pop-up will appear asking you to review the trade summary and fees.
- Press **Confirm** if all details are correct.

## vii. Transaction Completion

- The swap will be processed.
- You’ll receive confirmation once the trade is complete.

## Additional Tips

- Double-check the assets selected before submitting the transaction.
- Ensure you have enough balance to cover the network fee.
