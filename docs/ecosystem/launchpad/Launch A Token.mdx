# Launch a Token on PAW Chain

## i. Accessing the Onboarding Site

Open your preferred browser and visit: `https://onboarding.pawchain.net/`

## ii. Connect Your PAW Wallet

- Click the **Connect PAW Wallet** button in the upper right-hand corner
- Follow the prompts to connect your **PAW Wallet**
- Once connected, click the **Authenticate** button

## iii. Fill Out the Onboarding Form

Complete the onboarding form with the following token information:

- Primary network
- ERC-20 network address
- Any other networks where the same token contract exists
- Renounced contract status (yes/no)
- Team contact information
- Token logo

:::note

Double-check that your **Deployer PAW Wallet Address** is accurate.

:::

## iv. Submit

After reviewing the form:

- Press **Submit** at the bottom of the screen
- Allow time for review and authentication

You will be contacted using the information provided when your token is ready for deployment to **PAW Chain**.

# Bridging Your Token to PAW Chain

## v. Connect Your Wallet

- Open `https://wallet.pawchain.net`
- Click **Disconnected** in the top right corner of **PAW Wallet**
- Choose your preferred wallet (**MetaMask**, **WalletConnect**, etc.)
- Follow the prompts to connect

## vi. Select a Network

In the pop-up window, select the network you intend to bridge from.

## vii. Open the Settings Menu

- Click your **PAW Wallet address** in the top right
- Select **Settings**

## viii. Enable Developer Mode

- Scroll to the bottom and press [**Developer Mode**](/ecosystem/wallet/developer-mode)
- A pop-up will ask to confirm the action â€” press **Yes** to enable

## ix. Start the Bridge

- Press the **Bridge** button
- Select your token in the **Bridge From** dropdown

### Bridging Instructions

- **For EVM chains**: Choose the full token amount to bridge in
- **For Solana**: First, send `1 token` to the **PAW Chain deployer contract** on Solana
  - Afterward, bridge in the remaining amount

## x. Bridge Approval

If this is your first time bridging the token:

- Click **Approve** to grant the bridge permission to access your token
- Confirm in both **PAW Wallet** and your connected wallet

After approval, the **Bridge** button will reappear.

## xi. Complete the Bridge

- Press **Bridge** again to confirm and finalise the transaction
- Repeat this process for each network you want your token deployed on

> **Note**
> All EVM chains must use the **same token contract address** across chains for compatibility.
