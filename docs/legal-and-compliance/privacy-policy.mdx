# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

PAW Chain respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with PAW Chain.

## 2. Information We Collect

PAW Chain operates as a decentralized blockchain network and does not directly collect personal information. However, when engaging with PAW Chain services, third-party integrations, or community interactions, the following types of information may be processed:

- **Blockchain Data**: Publicly available transaction details recorded on the PAW Chain ledger.
- **User Communications**: Information provided when contacting support or participating in community discussions.
- **Technical Data**: IP addresses, device information, and analytics collected by third-party services for security and network performance.

## 3. How We Use Information

Since PAW Chain is a decentralized network, we do not directly process user data. However, interactions with our website, community platforms, and third-party service providers may involve data processing for:

- Network security and fraud prevention.
- Technical improvements and troubleshooting.
- Community engagement and communication.
- Compliance with legal obligations.

## 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

PAW Chain does not sell or share personal information. However, third-party services interacting with PAW Chain, such as wallet providers, analytics tools, or community platforms, may collect and process user data based on their own policies.

## 5. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect PAW Chain's infrastructure. However, users are responsible for securing their wallets, private keys, and personal devices.

## 6. User Rights and Control

Since PAW Chain does not store personal data, users have full control over their blockchain transactions. However, any data shared with third parties is subject to their privacy policies.

## 7. Updates to This Policy

PAW Chain may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on our official channels.

## 8. Contact Information

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [team@pawchain.net](mailto:team@pawchain.net).

By using PAW Chain, you acknowledge and accept the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
