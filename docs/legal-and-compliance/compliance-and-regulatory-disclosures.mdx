# Compliance and Regulatory Disclosures

## 1. Introduction

PAW Chain is committed to maintaining compliance with applicable laws and regulations governing blockchain technology and decentralized networks. This Compliance and Regulatory Disclosures document provides transparency regarding PAW Chain’s legal and regulatory considerations.

## 2. Regulatory Status

PAW Chain operates as a decentralized blockchain network and does not engage in activities requiring regulatory licensing in most jurisdictions. However, users must comply with their local regulations when utilizing PAW Chain’s services.

## 3. Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Know Your Customer (KYC) Policy

- PAW Chain does not directly conduct KYC or AML procedures as it is a decentralized blockchain network.
- Third-party services, including exchanges and wallet providers, may implement their own KYC/AML policies.
- Users are responsible for adhering to AML and KYC regulations in their respective jurisdictions.

## 4. Tax Compliance

Users are responsible for complying with applicable tax laws in their respective jurisdictions concerning transactions conducted on PAW Chain.

## 5. Data Privacy and Security

PAW Chain does not collect, store, or process personal data. Users interact with the blockchain in a pseudonymous manner, and all transaction data is publicly accessible on the ledger. Any data collection by third-party services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

## 6. Legal Jurisdiction

PAW Chain is a decentralized, globally accessible network. Users acknowledge that their use of PAW Chain is subject to the laws applicable in their jurisdiction.

## 7. Risk Disclosure

- Blockchain technology carries inherent risks, including but not limited to security vulnerabilities, regulatory changes, and market volatility.
- Users should exercise caution and conduct their own due diligence before engaging in transactions on PAW Chain.

## 8. Updates and Amendments

PAW Chain reserves the right to update this Compliance and Regulatory Disclosures document as necessary. Changes will be communicated through official channels.

## 9. Contact Information

For any compliance-related inquiries, please contact PAW Chain directly [team@pawchain.net](mailto:team@pawchain.net).

By using PAW Chain, you acknowledge and agree to these compliance and regulatory requirements.
