### Methods {#methods}

Use the PAW Chain swap platform ([**www.swap.pawchain.net**](http://www.swap.pawchain.net/)) or [Uniswap](http://www.uniswap.org) to acquire $PAW seamlessly.

  There are several ways to obtain PAW Coin ($PAW):

  1. **Trade/Obtain on Exchanges**: PAW can be obtained on various supported exchanges that list the token. Check the PAW Chain website for a list of exchanges that support $PAW trading.

  3. **Participate in Community Events**: PAW Chain periodically holds giveaways and other events where you can earn PAW Coin.
